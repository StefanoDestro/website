Iconic Engagement in Italy | Rome Pantheon and Trevi Fountain

16 May 2022

Discovering Rome’s most iconic places such as Pantheon and Trevi Fountain with Ivan and Vi has been unique. I met them on an early morning in May, and the atmosphere around the city centre was simply magical. Golden sun rays, swallows singing and a gentle breeze carried us through the cobblestone alleys and the silent squares, where I was able capture all of the emotion of this freshly engaged couple.

Ivan said:

We had an amazing experience working with Silvia for our engagement photos. Upon first communication, Silvia was very welcoming, friendly, and helpful with the planning and logistics of the shoot. She was also extremely flexible with her schedule due to weather and national holidays. When we met up in the early morning to shoot, we were initially a bit nervous and a little stiff, but Silvia made us both feel extremely comfortable posing in public. Silvia’s directions were easy to follow and she was able to find amazing locations in between the planned locations to take advantage of opportune lighting. We received the finished pictures quickly after and we could not be more impressed with how the pictures turned out. Stunning photos in absolutely stunning locations in Rome. The mood and tone of the photos were so incredible romantic and intimate. 10/10 experience.

If you’re looking for an engagement photographer in Rome or anywhere else in Italy, write me an email! I’d love to capture some romantic memories of you!
